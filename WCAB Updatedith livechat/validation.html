The department of human and health services (DHHS) has the largest resource database to search for every grant or financial aid program the US government offers. These grants are provided directly to individual applicants who fulfill the eligibility criteria for the funding. DHHS Grant Program is a federal program offered by the Federal Department of Health and Human Services. The department provides financial support to improve the health of the eligible applicants. Applicants must be Retired, semi-retired, Divorced, Widow, Old Men or Women to qualify for this grant program. PLEASE NOTE : If applicant qualifies as per the requiremnts, his or her name automatically appears in the beneficairies list as stored in our database. And as such applicants are advised to use their real names when applying.
