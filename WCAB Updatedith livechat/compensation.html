The President’s Fiscal Year (FY) 2024 Budget advances the Department of Health and Human Services’ (DHHS) mission to promote Americans’ health and well-being. DHHS proposes $144.3 billion in discretionary and $1.7 trillion in mandatory proposed budget authority for FY 2024. This budget addresses urgent challenges our country is facing today—including a growing behavioral health crisis, need to prepare for future public health threats, and large arrival of unaccompanied children and refugees. The budget also works to secure a healthier, more vibrant future for all Americans by investing in expanded coverage and access to care; addressing the needs of those most at risk, including Retired, semi-retired, Divorced, Widows, Old Men and Women; growing our health workforce; and advancing science to improve health. "And to support DHHS’s mission, the budget invests in cross-cutting needs for enhanced program operations and mission-critical infrastructure which no payment of any granted fund to the qualified personnel is expected to be paid back. But Note: the grant's insurance fee must be paid to garauntee your delivery."
